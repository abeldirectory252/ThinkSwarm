### Digital Payment Debate Summary

* **Telebirr:** Opposes forced interoperability; wants to protect its advantage.
* **M-Pesa:** Supports interoperability to compete fairly.
* **NBE:** Approves phased interoperability with controls.
* **Banks:** Demand stronger oversight.
* **Rural users:** Want affordable and easy payments.

### 12-Month Impact

| Stakeholder | Outcome                           |
| ----------- | --------------------------------- |
| Telebirr    | Remains leader but faces pressure |
| M-Pesa      | Gains market share                |
| Banks       | Expand digital services           |
| Users       | Benefit from easier transfers     |

**Result:** More competition, better access, and faster digital payment growth.
