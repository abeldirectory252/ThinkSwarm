### Ethiopia's Digital Payment Wars: Telebirr vs. Safaricom

Ethiopia's digital payments market is becoming competitive as **Telebirr** (Ethio Telecom) faces **M-Pesa** (Safaricom Ethiopia). Telebirr leads with a large user base and nationwide reach, while M-Pesa relies on its proven model and urban expansion. The **National Bank of Ethiopia** is considering rules on interoperability, remittances, and regulation that could reshape the market. Key questions are whether M-Pesa can catch up, how banks will respond, and whether digital payments will expand into rural areas.
